MON Beach Resort is located at Calungute in the district of MAPUSA about 30 km away from Panaji.Goa.Calungate Beach(Beach of the Whispering Breeze) nestled amongst tall casuarinas trees that offers restful shade for the beach.

The Resort is approximately 50-minute drive from Margoa Airport in Goa. Indigo Airlines, Spicejet and Air Asia are frequently fly to Goa connected with all the major cities in India.

Train service is also available from Bengaluru, Mumbai & via Southern and Western. For passengers from the central, train service is also available from Delhi to Goa, Pune via Mumbai. Both journeys will take about 12-hour.

Express bus services are also available from all major cities in India to Panaji(18 km) or Mapusa(5 km) away from the resort.
